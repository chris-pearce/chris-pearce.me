Hello, I’m Chris Pearce, a Front end designer on the product team at Campaign Monitor, based in Sydney, Australia.

I love the craft of building quality User Interfaces and care deeply about the user experience, accessibility, and maintainability of everything I build, and this shows in my work.

I’m really passionate about working on internal design systems with other like minded peers where I get to build highly consumable, accessible, and robust User Interface components. A system that allows engineers to focus on the feature logic not the view logic, that provides an organisation with consistent and accessible user experiences and where new product features can be delivered fast whilst still maintaining a high level of quality. Another passion of mine is CSS architecture and writing CSS at scale.

What else? I like to hang out with Kate Levin and try not to discuss our day jobs too much. Spend time with my daughter Matilda. Add to my trainer collection. Drink English Breakfast tea. Dream about owning a muscle car. And spend way too much time fussing over the details.
